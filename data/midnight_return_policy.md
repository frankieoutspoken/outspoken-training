# Midnight Cosmetics Return Policy

## Return Window
- Returns are accepted within **7 days** of delivery date.
- The delivery date is when the order status changes to "Delivered."

## Conditions
- Items must be **unopened** and in original packaging for a full refund.
- **Opened items** can only be exchanged for the same product in a different shade, not refunded.
- **Sale items** are final sale — no returns or exchanges.

## Exceptions
- If the item arrived **damaged**, the customer receives a full refund regardless of all other rules.
- Damaged item claims must include a photo of the damage.

## Process
1. Customer must provide their **order ID** to initiate a return.
2. Agent looks up the order to verify purchase date and eligibility.
3. If eligible, provide the customer with return instructions.
4. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5-7 business days** after the item is received.

## Escalation
- If the customer disputes the policy or requests a manager, collect their **name and email** and escalate to a human agent.
