# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping
**How long does shipping take?**
Standard shipping is 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional $8. Free standard shipping on orders over $50.

**Do you ship internationally?**
We currently ship to the US, Canada, and UK. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the customer's responsibility.

**Can I change my shipping address after ordering?**
Address changes can be made within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, the order enters fulfillment and cannot be modified. Contact support immediately if you need a change.

## Products
**Are your products cruelty-free?**
Yes. Midnight Cosmetics holds Leaping Bunny certification. We do not test on animals at any stage of product development, and neither do our suppliers.

**Are your products vegan?**
Most of our products are vegan. The exceptions are products containing beeswax or carmine. Each product page lists all ingredients and indicates whether it's vegan-friendly.

**What is your shade range?**
Our Lunar Glow Foundation is available in 24 shades across light, medium, tan, and deep categories. Our Midnight Matte Lipstick comes in 18 shades. We're continuously expanding our shade offerings based on customer feedback.

**Do you have products for sensitive skin?**
Yes. Our Nova Hydrating Primer and Lunar Glow Foundation are both formulated for sensitive skin. They're fragrance-free, hypoallergenic, and dermatologist-tested. We recommend patch testing any new product.

## Orders & Account
**How do I track my order?**
You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also check your order status by providing your order ID to our customer service agent.

**Can I cancel my order?**
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled. You may return it after delivery per our return policy.

**Do you offer gift cards?**
Yes! Digital gift cards are available in $25, $50, $75, and $100 denominations. They're delivered via email and never expire.

## Sustainability
**Is your packaging recyclable?**
Yes. All our packaging is either recyclable or part of our refill program. Compacts, palettes, and lipstick tubes can be returned to us for recycling through our Midnight Returns program — we'll even give you a $5 credit for each item returned.

**What is the Midnight Returns recycling program?**
Send back 5 empty Midnight Cosmetics containers and receive a $25 credit toward your next purchase. We partner with TerraCycle to ensure all returned packaging is properly recycled or upcycled.
