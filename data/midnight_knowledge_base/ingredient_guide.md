# Midnight Cosmetics Ingredient Guide

## Hyaluronic Acid
A moisture-binding ingredient that can hold up to 1000x its weight in water. Found in our Lunar Glow Foundation. It plumps skin, reduces the appearance of fine lines, and keeps makeup looking fresh throughout the day rather than settling into creases. Works for all skin types but especially beneficial for dehydrated and mature skin.

## Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)
A versatile ingredient that minimizes pores, evens skin tone, and strengthens the skin barrier. Found in our Nova Hydrating Primer. It helps control oil production without drying out the skin, making it ideal for people who struggle with both oiliness and dehydration. Also helps fade dark spots over time.

## Vitamin E
An antioxidant that protects skin from environmental damage and keeps lips soft. Found in our Midnight Matte Lipstick. It prevents the drying effect that most long-wear lip formulas cause, so you get 12-hour wear without the discomfort. Also helps heal chapped lips over time.

## Squalane
A lightweight, non-comedogenic oil derived from olives. Found in our Nova Hydrating Primer. It mimics your skin's natural oils, so it absorbs quickly without feeling greasy. Excellent for dry and sensitive skin types. Unlike heavier oils, squalane won't clog pores or cause breakouts.

## Peptides
Short chains of amino acids that signal your skin to produce more collagen. Found in our Nebula Lip Gloss for a natural plumping effect without irritation. Unlike harsh lip plumpers that use capsaicin or menthol to create a stinging sensation, peptides work gradually to improve lip fullness and smooth fine lines around the mouth.

## What We Don't Use
Midnight Cosmetics products are formulated without parabens, sulfates, phthalates, and synthetic fragrances. We do not test on animals and hold Leaping Bunny certification. All our packaging is recyclable or refillable.
