# Skincare Routines for Every Skin Type

## Morning Routine for Oily Skin
Start with a gentle foaming cleanser to remove overnight oil buildup. Follow with a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer — skip heavy creams. Apply SPF 30 or higher. For makeup, use a mattifying primer like our Nova Hydrating Primer (despite the name, its silicone-free formula works beautifully on oily skin without adding shine). Set everything with a translucent powder to control shine throughout the day.

## Morning Routine for Dry Skin
Use a cream-based cleanser that won't strip moisture. Layer a hydrating serum with hyaluronic acid, then a rich moisturizer. SPF is non-negotiable. For makeup days, a hydrating primer creates a smooth canvas and prevents foundation from clinging to dry patches. Look for foundations with built-in skincare ingredients like hyaluronic acid for all-day comfort.

## Evening Routine for Combination Skin
Double cleanse: start with an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup, then a water-based cleanser for a deep clean. Apply a balancing toner to even out oily and dry zones. Use a lightweight moisturizer on oily areas (T-zone) and a richer cream on dry areas (cheeks). Consider a gentle exfoliant 2-3 times per week to keep pores clear.

## Sensitive Skin Tips
Patch test everything. Avoid fragrances, alcohol, and harsh actives. Look for products with soothing ingredients like niacinamide, centella asiatica, and ceramides. When trying new makeup, introduce one product at a time and wait 48 hours before adding another. Our Nova Hydrating Primer is formulated specifically for sensitive skin with minimal ingredients.
